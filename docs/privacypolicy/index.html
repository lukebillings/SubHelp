Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026

SubHelp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our SubHelp mobile application ("App") on iOS devices. This policy applies globally and includes provisions required under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Subscription data: Names, prices, payment frequencies, renewal dates, and categories of subscriptions you add to the App.
  • Device settings: Currency preference, notification preferences, and other in-app settings you configure.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to SubHelp Premium, Apple may share transaction information with us (e.g., subscription status, renewal date) in accordance with Apple's App Store terms.
  • Device information: We may receive basic device information such as operating system version to ensure app compatibility.

2. Data Storage

Your subscription data (subscriptions you track and your cancellation history in the App) and certain app settings are stored on your device using standard iOS storage (e.g., UserDefaults). In addition, the App uses Apple’s iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync that same subscription data between your own devices signed into the same Apple ID with iCloud enabled.

We do not operate our own servers for storing your subscription list. iCloud sync is provided by Apple; Apple’s privacy policy and iCloud terms apply to data processed through iCloud: apple.com/legal/privacy. If iCloud is turned off or unavailable, the App continues to work with data stored locally on the device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App's core functionality (tracking subscriptions, calculating totals, scheduling renewal reminders).
  • To process and manage your SubHelp Premium subscription.
  • To improve the App and fix technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EEA, UK, and other GDPR-applicable jurisdictions, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the App and SubHelp Premium services you have agreed to.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our services, preventing fraud, and ensuring security, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent: Where we expressly ask for your consent for specific processing (e.g., optional analytics or marketing).
  • Legal obligation: Where we must process data to comply with applicable law.

5. Your Rights (Including GDPR Rights)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing in certain circumstances.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

You can remove subscription data from the App using Reset all data in Settings (within the App), or by deleting the App from your device. Data in your iCloud Key-Value store is managed by Apple and may persist according to your Apple ID and iCloud settings until you remove it or replace it (for example by resetting App data or managing storage through your Apple account). We do not maintain a separate copy of your subscription list on our systems. To exercise other rights, or to request a copy of any data we hold (outside what is on your device and Apple systems), please contact us using the details below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfil the purposes described in this policy. Locally stored data on your device remains until you delete the App, use in-app reset, or clear that data. Synced subscription data in iCloud Key-Value Storage remains according to Apple's retention and your iCloud account settings. For purchase and account-related data processed by Apple, retention follows Apple’s policies and applicable law (e.g., tax, dispute resolution).

7. International Transfers

Where your data is stored in iCloud, it may be processed on Apple’s infrastructure in various countries in accordance with Apple’s terms and privacy policy. If we transfer any personal data we hold outside the EEA, UK, or your home jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., standard contractual clauses, adequacy decisions) as required by applicable law, including GDPR.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction, such as 16 in some EEA countries). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. Data stored on your device is protected by your device's security (e.g., passcode, biometrics). When iCloud sync is used, Apple secures data in transit and at rest according to Apple's security practices. We do not receive or host a copy of your subscription list on infrastructure we operate.

10. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: For SubHelp Premium purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Apple iCloud (Key-Value Storage): To sync your subscription data between your devices linked to your Apple ID. Subject to Apple's iCloud and privacy terms.
  • Apple Push Notifications: For renewal reminders, if you enable them.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by other reasonable means. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For questions, to exercise your rights, or to lodge a complaint, please contact us:

  • By email: subhelp@outlook.com
  • Via the in-app support or contact option, if available

If you are in the EEA or UK and have concerns we cannot resolve, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, or your national data protection authority).
